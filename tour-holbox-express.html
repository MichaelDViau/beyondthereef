Holbox Experience

Private Speedboat · 3 Islands · Golf Cart · Beach Club Open Bar

🏝️ Private Speedboat🌊 3 Islands🛺 Golf Cart🍹 Open Bar⏱ Full Day (10 hrs)👥 Private
About This Experience

Experience Holbox in a more exclusive and unforgettable way, from start to finish.

Your day is designed to feel smooth, effortless, and far from the typical crowded route. Instead of waiting in long lines for the ferry, you head straight to the water and board your private speed boat.

From the very beginning, it feels different.

You glide across the turquoise waters toward Holbox Island, stopping along the way to explore two beautiful islands. Quiet, untouched, and surrounded by nature, these stops give you time to walk, take photos, and enjoy places that most visitors rush through or never even see.

No crowds, no pressure, just time to take it all in.

As you arrive in Holbox, the pace slows down even more.

A golf cart is waiting for you, the best way to explore the island’s sandy streets and relaxed atmosphere. No cars, no noise, just a laid-back island vibe that feels completely different from the mainland.

You will spend time discovering the charm of Holbox downtown, with colorful streets, local spots, and a unique energy that makes the island so special.

Then it is time to truly relax.

You head to a beach club where everything is set for you. Enjoy great food, comfortable seating, and direct access to one of the most beautiful beaches in the region. Soft white sand, shallow crystal-clear water, and plenty of time to swim, unwind, or simply do nothing at all.

Everything flows naturally throughout the day. No waiting, no rushing, no unnecessary stops. Just a well-planned experience where every moment feels easy and enjoyable.

This is not just a trip to Holbox. It is a smoother, more comfortable, and more elevated way to experience it.
